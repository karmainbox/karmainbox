FAQ

How do I upgrade?
Inside the extension, you'll see an "Upgrade to Pro" button. Payment is handled securely via the Chrome Web Store or Stripe.

What happens if I cancel?
You keep all your data. You just lose access to AI summaries and multi-inbox support.

Is there a trial?
Yes! Most features are free already. We may offer limited-time trials of Pro in future updates.
