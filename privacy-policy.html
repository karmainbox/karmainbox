Privacy Policy for Karma Inbox

Last updated: August 3, 2025

Privacy First: Karma Inbox is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your emails never leave your device, and we don't collect any personal information.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Karma Inbox does NOT collect, store, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your email content or messages
  • Your personal information or identity
  • Your browsing history or usage patterns
  • Any data about your contacts or email recipients
  • Analytics or tracking information

2. How Karma Inbox Works

Local Processing Only:

  • All email analysis happens entirely on your device
  • Karma scores are calculated locally in your browser
  • Behavioral analysis uses only local data processing
  • No email data is sent to our servers or any external services

3. Data Storage

Local Storage:

  • User preferences (AI provider choice, karma thresholds) stored locally in your browser
  • Cached email metadata (sender, subject, timestamps) stored locally for performance
  • All data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the extension

Secure Storage:

  • API keys are encrypted using AES-GCM encryption before local storage
  • No API keys or sensitive data are transmitted to our servers

4. Third-Party Services (Optional)

Gmail API:

  • Used only to read email metadata (sender, subject, timestamps) for karma calculations
  • Required for core functionality
  • Follows Google's privacy policies

AI Providers (Optional):

  • Only used if you choose to enable AI features
  • Requires your own API keys from chosen providers (OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, or local Ollama)
  • Email summaries are generated using your direct API connection
  • We don't have access to or store any API communications
  • Each provider has their own privacy policy for API usage

5. Permissions Explained

Required Permissions:

  • Gmail access: To read email metadata and inject karma indicators
  • Storage: To cache data locally and store preferences
  • Identity: For secure Gmail OAuth authentication
  • Active Tab: To display karma indicators in Gmail interface

Optional Permissions (requested when needed):

  • AI Provider domains: Only if you choose to use AI features
  • Localhost: Only if you choose to use local Ollama AI

6. Data Security

  • All sensitive data is encrypted before local storage
  • No data transmission to external servers beyond necessary API calls
  • OAuth tokens are securely managed through Chrome's identity system
  • Extension follows Chrome Web Store security policies

7. Your Rights and Control

  • You can revoke permissions at any time through Chrome settings
  • You can delete all local data by uninstalling the extension
  • You control which AI providers (if any) to use
  • No account creation or registration required

8. Children's Privacy

Karma Inbox does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The extension is designed to work with existing Gmail accounts and does not require or collect age information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last modified" date.

10. Open Source

Karma Inbox may be open-sourced in the future, allowing full transparency of data handling practices through code review.

11. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Karma Inbox's privacy practices:

  • Email: karmamailinbox@gmail.com
Summary: Karma Inbox prioritizes your privacy by processing all data locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or have access to your personal information or email content.
